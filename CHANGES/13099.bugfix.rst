Fixed ``BaseRequest.text()`` and ``BaseRequest.post()`` (the
``application/x-www-form-urlencoded`` branch) to raise
``HTTPUnsupportedMediaType`` (HTTP 415) when the request body is not valid
for the declared charset, instead of leaking a bare ``UnicodeDecodeError``.
A missing-charset body with invalid UTF-8 bytes was previously a 500.
